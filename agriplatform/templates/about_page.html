About AgriConnect

Connecting Farmers, Extension Workers, and Markets

Our Story

AgriConnect was founded with a mission to empower farmers in Malawi through digital solutions. By bridging the gap between farmers, agricultural experts, and markets, we aim to improve productivity, access to information, and overall livelihoods.

Our Mission

To connect farmers with reliable information, resources, and opportunities for sustainable farming.

Our Vision

A future where every farmer has the tools and knowledge to thrive in the modern agricultural economy.
