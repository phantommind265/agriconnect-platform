What We Do

We help Malawian farmers and extension workers connect, learn, trade, and make data‑driven decisions — all in one place.

How we create value

AgriConnect blends messaging, market access, and agronomy know‑how with a smart assistant. We bring reliable local data to your fingertips and make it simple to act — whether that’s finding buyers, asking an expert, or planning for next week’s weather.
All features are designed for low data usage and simple, reliable access even on basic devices.
